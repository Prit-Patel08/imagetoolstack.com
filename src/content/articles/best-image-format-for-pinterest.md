Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Pinterest**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Constraints:** Vertical aspect ratio (2:3 or 1000 x 1500px). Max 20MB file size.

## Why this matters for Pinterest
Pinterest feeds are highly saturated, meaning your Pins need to stand out. Vertical orientation is crucial. Use JPG for lifestyle and product photography. If your Pin relies heavily on typography, infographics, or graphical elements, PNG will prevent the text from looking fuzzy.

Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Facebook Ads**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Constraints:** 1080 x 1080 pixels. Less than 20% text in the image is recommended for best delivery.

## Why this matters for Facebook Ads
Facebook's ad delivery system optimizes images dynamically. For photographic ads, JPG is ideal. For illustrative ads or ads with text, PNG ensures sharp edges and crisp typography that won't look blurry on high-density mobile screens.

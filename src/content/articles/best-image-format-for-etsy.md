Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Etsy**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Constraints:** Recommended size: 2000px on the shortest side. Max file size: 1MB recommended for fast loading.

## Why this matters for Etsy
Etsy relies on highly visual product displays to attract buyers. While PNG is great if you have graphics or text, JPG is the most reliable format for high-resolution product photography because it captures complex lighting and textures while keeping the file size manageable.

Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Shopify**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** WebP for maximum speed
- **Constraints:** Max 20 Megapixels, max 20MB file size. Recommended ratio: Square (1:1) for products.

## Why this matters for Shopify
Shopify automatically serves images in WebP format when supported by the browser, but uploading highly optimized WebP or JPG files initially prevents artifacting. Faster load times directly correlate to higher conversion rates, so prioritizing compression over raw quality is essential for e-commerce.

Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Amazon**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG with pure white background
- **Constraints:** Pure white background (RGB 255,255,255). At least 1000px on the longest side for zoom functionality.

## Why this matters for Amazon
Amazon has strict image requirements for main product listings. Your primary image must have a pure white background and cannot contain text, logos, or watermarks. JPG is the mandated standard as it perfectly handles product photography without the overhead of transparency channels.

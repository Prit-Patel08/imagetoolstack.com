Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for WhatsApp**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG
- **Constraints:** Max 16MB for general media. Images are heavily compressed automatically by WhatsApp.

## Why this matters for WhatsApp
WhatsApp aggressively compresses every image sent through standard chat. Uploading a high-quality JPG is recommended because the algorithm handles it predictably. If you send a PNG, WhatsApp may convert it or struggle to compress it efficiently, potentially leading to quality loss.

Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Twitch Emotes**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** PNG
- **Constraints:** Must have transparent background. Sizes: 28x28, 56x56, and 112x112 px (or auto-resize up to 4096x4096px, 1MB max).

## Why this matters for Twitch Emotes
Twitch emotes absolutely require a transparent background to overlay correctly against the dark or light chat interface. PNG is the only acceptable static format. For animated emotes, GIF is required, strictly keeping under the 1MB limit.

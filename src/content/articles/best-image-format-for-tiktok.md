Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for TikTok**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG or WebP for profile and cover images
- **Constraints:** Profile picture: minimum 20x20, recommended 200x200. Covers should be 1080 x 1920.

## Why this matters for TikTok
While TikTok is a video platform, your profile picture and video thumbnail covers matter immensely. JPG provides the sharpest quality for profile photos, ensuring your branding looks legitimate and professional.

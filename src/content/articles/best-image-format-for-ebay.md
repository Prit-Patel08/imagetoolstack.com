Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for eBay**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG
- **Constraints:** Minimum 500px on the longest side. Max 12MB. No borders or text overlays.

## Why this matters for eBay
eBay automatically optimizes uploaded images. JPG is the universally accepted standard that ensures your product photos load quickly on both desktop and mobile, reducing buyer friction and improving your listing's conversion rate.

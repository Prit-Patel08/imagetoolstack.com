Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Instagram Ads**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Constraints:** 1080 x 1080 pixels (1:1 ratio) for feed. Max file size: 30MB.

## Why this matters for Instagram Ads
Instagram is a visual-first platform. While standard posts are converted to JPG, if your ad contains heavy text overlays or graphics, uploading a PNG can sometimes prevent the text compression artifacts that Instagram's algorithm might otherwise introduce.

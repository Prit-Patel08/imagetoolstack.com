Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for Discord**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** PNG for transparent emotes, JPG for standard chat images
- **Constraints:** File size limit: 25MB (free), 50MB (Nitro Basic), 500MB (Nitro). Emote size: 128x128 max.

## Why this matters for Discord
Discord heavily relies on transparent backgrounds for custom server emotes and stickers, making PNG the undisputed champion for graphics. However, if you are just sharing screenshots or memes in chat, sticking to JPG will ensure faster upload times and use less data for mobile users.

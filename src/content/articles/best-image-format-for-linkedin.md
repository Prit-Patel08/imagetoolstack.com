Choosing the right image format is critical for maximizing visual quality and avoiding unexpected compression artifacts on social and e-commerce platforms. This guide breaks down the **Best Image Format for LinkedIn**.

## Official Requirements
- **Recommended Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Constraints:** Profile: 400x400px. Banner: 1584x396px. Max 8MB.

## Why this matters for LinkedIn
LinkedIn is a professional network where image clarity equals credibility. Because LinkedIn's compression can be quite harsh on JPGs with text or logos, PNG is strongly recommended for company logos and banner images to maintain crisp edges.
